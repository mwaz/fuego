Fuego

Fire-Fuego is a fire mapping tool crafted for fire managers
to assist them fight the danger and spread of wildfires.
Unlike other fire mapping tools it combines satellite data,
IoT sensor data and fire reports from individuals to predict,
detect and navigate fire affected areas.
